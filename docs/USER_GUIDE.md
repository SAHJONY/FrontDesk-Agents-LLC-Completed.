# FrontDesk Agents - User Guide

**Welcome to FrontDesk Agents!** This guide will help you get started with the platform and make the most of its powerful features.

## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1. Logging In

- Access the platform at [https://frontdeskagents.com/login](https://frontdeskagents.com/login)
- Enter your email and password to log in.

### 1.2. Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard is the central hub for all your front office operations. It provides a real-time overview of:

- **Key Metrics:** Total calls, answer rate, total bookings, lead conversion, and MRR.
- **Quick Actions:** Easily access common tasks like adding a lead, booking an appointment, or sending a message.
- **Recent Activity:** A live feed of all recent events, including calls, messages, and bookings.

## 2. Core Features

### 2.1. Scheduling & Appointments

- **Book Appointments:** Schedule appointments directly from the dashboard, CRM, or call logs.
- **Manage Calendar:** View and manage your team's availability.
- **Reminders:** Automated SMS and email reminders are sent to customers before their appointments.
- **Reschedule/Cancel:** Customers can easily reschedule or cancel appointments via SMS or email.

### 2.2. CRM & Lead Management

- **Lead Capture:** Leads are automatically captured from calls, SMS, emails, and web forms.
- **Pipeline Management:** Track leads through your sales pipeline (New, Qualified, Proposal, Won, Lost).
- **Lead Scoring:** Leads are automatically scored based on their engagement and demographics.
- **Notes & Tasks:** Add notes and create tasks for each lead to stay organized.

### 2.3. Team Management

- **Workspaces:** Organize your business into multiple workspaces, each with its own settings and team members.
- **Roles & Permissions:** Assign roles to your team members (Owner, Admin, Manager, Agent, Viewer) to control access.
- **Invitations:** Invite new team members to your workspace via email.
- **Locations:** Manage multiple business locations, each with its own phone number, business hours, and services.

## 3. Communication Channels

### 3.1. SMS & Messaging

- **Two-Way SMS:** Engage in real-time SMS conversations with your customers.
- **Missed-Call Text-Back:** Automatically send a text message to customers whose calls you miss.
- **Templates:** Use pre-built templates for common messages like appointment reminders and follow-ups.
- **Campaigns:** Send targeted SMS campaigns to your customer lists.

### 3.2. Email Operations

- **Inbound Email:** All emails sent to your designated support/sales address are automatically ingested.
- **Auto-Classification:** Emails are automatically categorized (support, sales, billing) and routed to the right team member.
- **AI-Drafted Responses:** Let our AI draft responses to common inquiries for your approval.
- **Sequences:** Create multi-step email sequences for lead nurturing and follow-ups.

## 4. Analytics & Reporting

- **Dashboards:** Access real-time analytics on call volume, booking rates, lead conversion, and revenue.
- **Reports:** Generate daily, weekly, or monthly reports on all aspects of your business.
- **Custom Dashboards:** Create your own custom dashboards with the metrics that matter most to you.
- **CSV Export:** Export your data to CSV for further analysis.

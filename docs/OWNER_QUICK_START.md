## 5. Example Autonomous Workflows

### Inbound Lead Processing
1.  A new lead fills out a form on your website.
2.  The **Customer Acquisition Division** qualifies the lead using the BANT framework.
3.  The **Intelligence Division** enriches the lead with company data.
4.  A personalized outreach email is generated and sent by the **Email Operations Division**.
5.  The AI books a demo on your calendar.

### Customer Support Request
1.  A customer sends a support email.
2.  The **Email Operations Division** analyzes sentiment and priority.
3.  The **Customer Success Division** generates an immediate response.
4.  If needed, the issue is escalated to the **Technical Operations Division**.
5.  The entire interaction is logged in your CRM.
